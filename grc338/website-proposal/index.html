Introduction

Mint Salon and Spa

Mint Salon and Spa is a local, luxury hair salon located in San Luis Obispo, California. It consists of four talented hair stylists and two estheticians.

Target audience

Mint has a variety of clients that vary in age. However the one's who will utalize the site will be men and women who are looking for a new hairstylist or esthetician in the San Luis Obispo area. They are individuals who are needing a new salon to meet their everchanging desires.

Those who visit Mint's site will want to be able to view the services offered, check prices, and learn how they can book an appointment. Another goal would be to understand where the salon is located in San Luis Obispo since it is on a main road.
